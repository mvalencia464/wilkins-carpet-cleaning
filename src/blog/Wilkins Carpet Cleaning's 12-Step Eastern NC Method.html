The Exclusive Wilkins 12-Step Carpet Cleaning Process

Our state-of-the-art truck-mounted hot water extraction system, combined with our meticulous 12-step process, delivers results that have earned us a 4.8-star rating with over 50 Google reviews from satisfied Rocky Mount area customers.

Step 1Pre-Inspection

Our certified technician conducts a thorough walkthrough inspection with you to identify soiling conditions, carpet construction, and potential permanent stains. This crucial first step allows us to customize our cleaning approach for your specific carpet type and condition.

Step 2Pre-Vacuum

Your carpet is pre-vacuumed with commercial-grade equipment to remove bonded, insoluble dry soil that regular home vacuums can't extract. This step removes up to 85% of dry soil before the wet cleaning process begins.

Step 3Furniture Movement & Protection

Sofas, chairs, and tables are carefully moved to access all carpet areas. Larger pieces such as beds and dressers are left in place under our regular pricing structure. All furniture is protected with disposable blocks and tabs to prevent damage and staining.

Step 4Pre-Spot Treatment

Specific spots and stains are pre-treated with specialized solutions for maximum removal potential. Our Rocky Mount carpet cleaning technicians use professional-grade spotting agents not available to consumers.

Step 5Pre-Spray Application

High-traffic areas receive specialized pre-treatment with biodegradable traffic lane solutions. This step breaks down ground-in soil and prepares it for extraction.

Step 6Pre-Grooming

Your carpet is pre-groomed with professional carpet grooming equipment or rotary cleaning machines, depending on soil level. This mechanical action further loosens embedded traffic area soil that other Rocky Mount carpet cleaners often miss.

Step 7Hot Water Extraction & Rinse

Once soil has been properly loosened, our state-of-the-art truck-mounted hot water extraction system thoroughly flushes the carpet pile. Pressure and heat are carefully regulated to prevent over-wetting while maximizing soil removal.

Step 8pH Neutralization

The pH is balanced on every carpet and fabric cleaned. This is accomplished during the extract and rinse process or through post-spray application, ensuring no cleaning residue remains that could attract future soil.

Step 9Post-Spot Treatment

If any spots are not completely removed during the cleaning process, specialized spotting techniques are employed. Our technicians use advanced stain removal methods developed over 20+ years of experience.

Step 10Post-Grooming

The carpet pile is professionally groomed and set in one direction to aid the drying process and restore the carpet's original appearance and texture.

Step 11Speed Drying

High-velocity air movers are strategically placed on the carpet to significantly reduce drying time. Your Rocky Mount home's carpets will be dry and ready for normal use in hours, not days.

Step 12Post-Cleaning Inspection

Our technician conducts a final walkthrough with you to point out cleaning results and ensure you are completely satisfied with the work. We don't consider the job complete until you're thrilled with the results.
